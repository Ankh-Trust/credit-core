Credit 1.0.0.0
=====================

Intro
-----
The Credit(0AC) blockchain exists.

Setup
-----
Unpack the files into a directory and run credit-qt.exe.

Credit is the original Credit client and it builds the backbone of the network.
However, it downloads and stores the entire history of Credit transactions;
depending on the speed of your computer and network connection, the synchronization
process can take anywhere from a few hours to a day or more.
